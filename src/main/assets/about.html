FlightIntel
Nadeem Hasan <nhasan@nadmm.com>

This application makes use of the data from following sources:

A/FD — Airport/Facility data is provided by FADDS - Facility Aeronautical Data Distribution System, part of the NFDC - National Flight Data Center. This data is updated on a 56-day cycle.

Timezone information is populated in the data file using GeoNames.

FlightIntel is not in any way affiliated with FAA or any other government agency.

Privacy

FlightIntel requires access to your device's location to provide information about nearby airports and weather facilities. Your privacy is important to me. Therefore, I want to assure you that the location information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server. This app does not collect or store any personal identity data, ensuring that your location cannot be associated with a specific user.
